We believe on people's choice, and freedom. Choice to decide the size, format, color and feel of your indispensable handheld device. Freedom to escape the prison of large manufacturers design ideas, and their dogma that only them know what is best for you. Choice to make a statement on how you want to connect and be seen connected to the world. Freedom to build your personalized high quality product.

Moebius is your choice and your path to freedom.

The technology is mature enough to make a business model out of user preferences and choices in the mobile computing world. With simple basic decisions, like form factor, style, color and material you can really make a difference. In Moebius we take care of your decisions, counsel you when you need it, and show you what similar consumers have chosen and their feedback.

No matter if you are an individual consumer or a business, Moebius is your choice to enjoy high tech design at your fingertips

FAQ

Why Moebius
Have you noticed that no matter where you look at, you can broadly classify all the people around you by the brand and mobile device they use? That might seem a light choice, but that will mark you, stereotype you and, more importantly, influence your decisions on other matter as well as your lifestyle... even the people you can talk to! In Moebius we believe that people whould be free, unique and authentic. We want to return the freedom of choice on basic but very influential design factors to the user in the world of consumer electronics
What can I do to contribute to the Moebius project?
We are delighted that you have this question! We would love to hear from you what would you like to change in the device that consumes more of your attention time today: that beloved (or hated) pseudo slab device that connects you to your friends, work, loved ones.. basically to the world itself! If you like the ideas that we promulgate here, and you can afford it, we would love your contribution in $, so we can make this project a reality.
